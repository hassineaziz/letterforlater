Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. We protect your most precious memories with enterprise-grade security.

Last updated: January 2025

Enterprise Security

Bank-level encryption protects your letters

No Data Selling

Complete Control

1
Introduction

At LetterForLater, we understand that your letters contain your most precious memories and messages for loved ones. We are committed to protecting your privacy with enterprise-grade security and transparent practices.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our digital legacy letter service.

2
Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, and account settings
  • Two-factor authentication data (if enabled)
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)

Your Content

  • Letters and messages you create
  • Photos, videos, and audio attachments
  • Delivery schedules and recipient information
  • Trusted contact details for verification

Technical Data

  • Device information and IP address for security
  • Usage logs for service improvement and security
  • Analytics data (only with your consent)

3
How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

  • • Store and protect your letters
  • • Deliver messages at scheduled times
  • • Manage trusted contact verification
  • • Provide customer support

Security & Safety

  • • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • • Monitor for security threats
  • • Maintain service reliability
  • • Comply with legal requirements

Important: We never access the content of your letters unless required for technical support you've requested. Your messages remain private and encrypted.

4
Information Sharing

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing

We only share your information in these specific circumstances:

  • Trusted Contacts: Only when you've designated them to receive verification requests
  • Service Providers: Secure cloud storage and email delivery services under strict contracts
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

5
Data Security

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.

Access Controls

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access systems.

Regular Backups

Automatic backups protect your data with multiple redundant systems.

99.9% Uptime

Enterprise-grade infrastructure ensures your letters are always accessible.

6
Your Rights & Choices

Access & Download

Request a copy of all your data at any time

Correction & Updates

Update or correct your information anytime

Account Deletion

Request complete deletion of your account and data

Marketing Preferences

Opt out of marketing emails while keeping essential notifications

To exercise any of these rights: Contact us at privacy@letterforlater.com or through your account settings.

7
Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide our service. This ensures your letters can be delivered according to your wishes.

Retention Periods

  • • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • • Delivered Letters: Kept for 7 years for delivery confirmation
  • • Account Deletion: Complete removal within 30 days of request
  • • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

8
Cookies & Tracking

Essential Cookies

Required for authentication, security, and core functionality. Cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Optional cookies for service improvement. Only used with your explicit consent.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings or through our cookie consent banner.

9
International Users

LetterForLater is designed to serve families worldwide. We comply with international privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable regulations.

GDPR Compliance

European users have additional rights under GDPR, including data portability, the right to be forgotten, and the right to object to processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.
