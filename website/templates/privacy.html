01 Introduction

At LetterForLater, we understand that your letters contain your most precious memories and messages for loved ones. We are committed to protecting your privacy with enterprise-grade security and transparent practices.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our digital legacy letter service.

02 Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, and account settings
  • Two-factor authentication data (if enabled)
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)

Your Content

  • Letters and messages you create
  • Photos, videos, and audio attachments
  • Delivery schedules and recipient information
  • Trusted contact details for verification

Technical Data

  • Device information and IP address for security
  • Usage logs for service improvement and security
  • Website analytics data for service improvement
  • Newsletter subscription data

04 Information Sharing

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing

We only share your information in these specific circumstances:

  • Trusted Contacts: Only when you've designated them to receive verification requests
  • Service Providers: Secure cloud storage and email delivery services under strict contracts
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

05 Data Security

06 Your Sovereignty & Rights

download_for_offline

Archive Protocol (Data Export)

We believe in true data sovereignty. You have the right to request a complete, encrypted archive of all your letters, media, and metadata. This protocol ensures your voice is never locked into a single platform.

visibility_off

Deactivation Protocol

Instead of simple deletion, we offer a suspension option. Deactivation pauses your account but preserves your digital estate in a secure 'stasis' mode, protecting your letters from accidental loss during transitions.

delete_forever

Permanent Erasure

You have the 'Right to be Forgotten.' Upon a verified request, we will permanently purge all your data, including encrypted letters and keys, from our active servers within 30 days.

To exercise your rights: You may initiate these protocols directly from your Account Settings or by contacting our Data Protection Officer at support@letterforlater.com.

07 Data Retention & Cookies

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide our service. Active accounts retain data indefinitely. Delivered letters are kept for 7 years for delivery confirmation. Upon account deletion, all data is completely removed within 30 days.

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies required for authentication and core functionality. With your consent, we use Google Analytics and Tag Manager to understand website usage. You can manage preferences via your browser or the cookie consent banner on our site.
